About Ani-Mahal Cares

Ani-Mahal Cares is a PH based non profit organization dedicated to animal welfare. It was established in September 2022. Our mission is to inspire others to help and rescue fur animals in a simple way by giving a token or donations.

"Let them know someone cares"

OUR MISSION: Is to rescue,rehome abused,abandoned,rehabilitate and neglected animals.

OUR VISION: It is to create an animal friendly community that gives kindness to all stray animals.

OUR INITIATIVES: Stray feeding Neuter Relief Drives
